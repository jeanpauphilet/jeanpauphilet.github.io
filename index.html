About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Management Science & Operations group at London Business School. My research interests lie at the intersection of optimization under uncertainty, machine learning, and statistics, and their application to healthcare and sustainable operations. I am very happy to collaborate with Analytics for a Better World.

I received a Ph.D. in Operations Reserch from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), under the supervision of Prof. Dimitris Bertsimas. I earned a Diplome d'ingenieur (MSc. equivalent) in Applied Mathematics from Ecole Polytechnique (Paris) and am part of the French Corps des Mines. Before joining MIT, I worked for one year as an analyst in the French Venture Capital firm Ventech.
